Mask Art Challenge

Design a protective mask with your favorite Nickelodeon characters on it! Having a mask will protect you from various germs floating through the air that people cough or sneeze. It will also protect anyone in your vicinity just in case you cough and sneeze!

Sanitizer Challenge

Design a label for hand sanitizer inspired by your favorite Nickelodeon show! Washing your hands frequently and for long enough will make sure germs aren’t traveling on you!
