Challenges

MaskArtChallenge

Design a protective mask with your favorite Nickelodeon characters on it! Having a mask will protect you from various germs floating through the air that people cough or sneeze. It will also protect anyone in your vicinity just in case you cough and sneeze!

SingingChallenge

Record yourself singing your favorite Nickelodeon theme song while you wash your hands! Washing your hands frequently and for long enough will make sure germs aren’t traveling on you!

WritingChallenge

Write about what you miss most about school, and what you are excited to learn about once you get back! Right now, you may hear adults say that we are doing something called “social distancing. ” We are social distancing in order to prevent the coronavirus from spreading person to person. Even though it is very hard to not leave your house and see other people, it is what is best to help stop the virus.
